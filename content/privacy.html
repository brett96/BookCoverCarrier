01 Information We Collect

We collect information you provide and information generated automatically as you use the Services.

Information you provide

When you register for demo access, we collect your name, work email address, mobile phone number, company or organization name, job title, and a description of your business type. We also collect the verification code you enter to confirm your identity, and any information you share when you contact us.

Information collected automatically

We record access activity related to your account — including registration events, verified sign-ins, and which demos you open, along with the associated date and time. We may also collect standard technical information such as browser type and device characteristics.

02 How We Use Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Create and manage your demo account and verify your identity through two-factor verification;
  • Provide, maintain, and secure access to the Services;
  • Understand how the demos are used and improve our products and content;
  • Respond to your inquiries and communicate with you about BookCover; and
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms.

03 How We Share Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service providers who perform functions on our behalf (such as hosting, email or SMS delivery, and analytics), under obligations to protect the information;
  • Legal and safety reasons, where disclosure is required by law or necessary to protect the rights, property, or safety of CercaLabs, our users, or others; and
  • Business transfers, in connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, subject to this Policy.

04 Cookies & Local Storage

The demo portal uses your browser's local storage to remember your registration, maintain your signed-in session, and record demo access activity on your device. This information is stored in your browser. You can clear it at any time through your browser settings; doing so will remove your saved session and require you to register or sign in again.

05 Health Information & Member Data

This demonstration portal does not collect, process, or display real health information. The data shown in the demos is fictitious and for illustration only.

In production deployments of BookCover, member health information is provided by and remains under the control of the health plan. In those engagements, CercaLabs acts as a business associate to the health plan and handles protected health information in accordance with the applicable Business Associate Agreement and governing law. This portal is not used to transmit real protected health information.

06 Data Security

We maintain administrative, technical, and physical safeguards designed to protect information against unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, however, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

07 Data Retention

We retain personal information for as long as needed to provide the Services, fulfill the purposes described in this Policy, and meet legal, accounting, or reporting requirements. We may retain demo access logs to understand usage and maintain the security of the Services.

08 Your Rights & Choices

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or restrict the use of your personal information, and to request a copy of it. You may also opt out of marketing communications at any time. To exercise any of these rights, please contact us. We will respond consistent with applicable law.

09 Children's Privacy

The Services are intended for business users and are not directed to children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us information, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

10 Third-Party Links

The Services may link to third-party websites or services that we do not control. This Policy does not apply to those third parties, and we encourage you to review their privacy practices.

11 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date above. Material changes will be communicated as required by applicable law. Your continued use of the Services after an update indicates your acceptance of the revised Policy.
